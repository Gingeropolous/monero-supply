As of today ... XMR have been generated. All of them were given to miners to incentive them to keep the network safe.
Unlike Bitcoins block reward Moneros block rewards will never drop to zero. Instead they will gradually drop until tail emission commences at the end of May 2022. At this point, rewards will be fixed at 0.6 XMR per block. 768 XMR will be created per day.
